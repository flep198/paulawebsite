Food Mood

UI/UX Design, 3rd semester, 2022/23

Food Mood is an idea for a digital recipe organizer app, with which you can keep all your favorite recipes in one place. You can collect recipes from all over the internet or create your own and organize them however you like. In the community you can see what your friends are cooking and get inspired by their favorite meals.
