Die Bausteine des Weingeschmacks

Digital Storytelling, 4th semester, 2023

Ever wondered what wine experts mean when they say they can taste unusual aromas like butter or wood? This uniquely designed webstory takes you on a journey to learn all about the flavor of wine and how it develops and changes throughout the wine making process. This project was realized in partnership with the Landesanstalt für Wein- und Gartenbau (LWG) in Veitshöchheim.
