About me

Hello, my name is Paula, and I'm thrilled to share my passion for design with you! As a 23-year-old communication design student at Hof University, I have immersed myself in the world of visual communication for the past four semesters. For my upcoming practical semester I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to an internship, where I can learn from experienced professionals and gain real-world experience in the industry. I am especially passionate about web design, UI/UX design and digital storytelling.
